About Us

Vtrack is a vehicle tracking system that provides real-time monitoring solutions to businesses and individuals.
Our platform utilizes advanced GPS technology and other cutting-edge tools to track and monitor vehicles, providing accurate and reliable data on their location, movements, speed, and other key metrics.

Our Company

Vtrack is highly customizable, allowing you to set up alerts and notifications for various events such as when a vehicle deviates from a pre-defined route, exceeds a certain speed limit, or enters/exits a specified area.

Our system is particularly useful for fleet management, logistics, and transportation industries, where it enables businesses to optimize their operations, improve safety and security, and reduce costs associated with vehicle maintenance and fuel consumption.

Additionally, Vtrack is also ideal for educational institutions that provide student transportation services, as it allows them to monitor and manage their vehicles and ensure the safety of their students during transit. With Vtrack, you can have peace of mind knowing that your vehicles and students are always in safe hands..
